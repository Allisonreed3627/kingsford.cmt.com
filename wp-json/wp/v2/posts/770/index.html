SAN DIEGO \u2014 Have you experienced an aerial phenomenon? If you ask some San Diegans that question, their answer would be \u201cyes.\u201d<\/p>\n

According to the National UFO Reporting Center<\/a> (NUFORC), several unidentified flying object (UFO) sightings in the region have been reported so far this year.<\/p>\n

NUFORC is a non-governmental, non-profit corporation that investigates UFO sightings. The center says it receives, records, corroborates and documents reports from individuals who believe they have witnessed an aerial phenomenon.<\/p>\n

Since it was founded in 1974, NUFORC says it has processed over 170,000 reports, which are listed online by date and location. The center explained that guaranteed anonymity to callers is a longstanding policy of the corporation. <\/p>\n

NUFORC makes available to the public all of its data in summary form with the time, location, duration of the sighting and a short summary describing the UFO.<\/p>\n

As of Aug. 6, 2023, there have been a total of 15 UFO sightings across San Diego County that have been reported to NUFORC this year.<\/p>\n
