As people made their way through the Marina Green Park during the last day of the annual Long Beach Pride Festival, they were ready to dance, watch drag shows \u2014 and celebrate the LGBTQ community.<\/p>\n

A rainbow of color washed over downtown Long Beach on Sunday afternoon, Aug. 6, with hundreds \u2014 many in extravagant, colorful and glittery outfits \u2014 enjoying the final day of Pride weekend, hours after the traditional parade marched down Ocean Boulevard<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Carlos Gaitan, from North Hollywood, was among the crowd Sunday, donning an ensemble that included four-inch heels with rainbow laces. Pride celebrations like the one in Long Beach provide spaces for many members of the LGBTQ community to dress like their authentic selves, he said.<\/p>\n
